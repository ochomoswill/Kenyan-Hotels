Five Star Hotels in Nairobi City

Nairobi is a vibrant city with fine-dining restaurants, modern shopping complexes and 5 star hotels. Since it is Kenya’s capital city, it is the main entry point for business and leisure travellers.

Nairobi’s 5 star hotels offer a variety of luxury services like concierge services, valet parking, airport pickup, Jacuzzi, private transport and tours, spa services, fitness centre and private swimming pools. You will definitely get VIP services by staying in 5 star hotels Nairobi.

Five star hotels are located in different parts of the city, which makes it easy for visitors to find lavish accommodation anytime. There is quite a number of luxury hotels near or within Nairobi's city centre.
